Ask questions about your Obsidian vault using AI.

Usage:
  /obsidian:ask <question>

Examples:
  /obsidian:ask "What did I write about quantum computing?"
  /obsidian:ask "Summarize my notes on machine learning"
  /obsidian:ask "What are my most recent notes about Python?"

This command:
- Searches your vault for relevant notes
- Uses Claude AI to understand context
- Provides answers based on your knowledge base
- Cites source notes in the response
